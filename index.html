About us
Since 1985, Dream Cakes has been baking delicious cakes from scratch, beautifully packaging them, and delivering them to our customers' doors. Over the years, we have delivered tens of thousands of delicious cakes throughout the country. We deliver for birthdays, corporate parties and holidays. We baked for clients, family and friends. And each cake is carefully made from scratch with only the finest, all-natural ingredients, just like you would use at home.
